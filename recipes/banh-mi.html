Banh Mi

Description

This delicious sandwich treat is easy to make in less than five minutes, as well as tasty!

Ingredients

  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 small cucumber
  • one half cup cilantro
  • 2 green chili peppers
  • one eighth cup rice wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 cup hot water
  • 12 ounces boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 footlong baguette
  • 3 table spoons mayonaisse

Directions

  1. Mine the garlic
  2. Peel the carrot and chop it into matchstick pieces
  3. Peel and slice the cucumber into ribbons
  4. Slice the green chillies and pick the cilantro leaves off stems
  5. Mix the sugar, rice wine vinegar, and salt with the hot water until it dissolves
  6. Pour the mixture over the Carrots and let it sit to pickle over 1 hour
  7. Chop the chicken meat into bite size pieces and place them in a bowl
  8. Pour everything over the chicken
  9. Heat a frying pan over medium-high and saute the marinated chicken until cooked. About 10 minutes.
  10. Slice open the baguette and scoop out some of the soft bread inside to create more room for the sandwich fillings.
  11. Place the cooked chicken inside the bread followed by carrots, cucumbers, cilantro, and chili peppers
  12. Slice the banh mi in half to make two servings and enjoy!
